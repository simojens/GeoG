Ecuador Metas

Ecuador has a white Google car equipped with a short and thick ("stubby") antenna.
Google Car
In Ecuador, commercial vehicles have license plates that are either solid orange or have an orange bar at the top.
License Plate
In Ecuador, bollards are quite common, which is unique in Latin America. Bollards are either round with two red stripes or flat with two red reflectors on a black background. While various other styles of bollards can be found in Ecuador, they typically maintain a consistent color scheme and style.
Bollards
Ecuador frequently uses road signs that are square with white background.
Street Sign
In Ecuador, most utility poles have a generic round and concrete design. However, there's another type of pole (the 'ladder pole') with numerous small indents resembling ladder steps. This type of pole is more or less unique to Ecuador within Latin America.
Poles
